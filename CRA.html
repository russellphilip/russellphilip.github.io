Campus For Change

Campus 4 Change is a student-led organization focused on empowering individuals to create sustainable social impact through local and global projects. By providing a platform for students to initiate and manage social initiatives, the organization fosters collaboration, leadership, and meaningful change.

Campus 4 Change is dedicated to creating meaningful and sustainable social change by supporting a wide range of local and global initiatives. The organization's primary goal is to enable members to actively engage in social projects that directly impact communities. By fostering a culture of efficiency and collaboration, Campus 4 Change ensures that resources are used effectively to maximize the positive outcomes of its projects.

The organization operates through a well-structured framework that divides responsibilities among five key executive areas: Finance & Law, Fundraising, Human Resources, External Communication, and Project Evaluation & Supervision. This structure ensures a seamless coordination of projects and events, with project leads working alongside department heads to streamline efforts. Support from academic and industry advisors, along with the involvement of alumni, strengthens the organization's long-term sustainability and impact.

In my role as both fundraising coordinator and managing director for the Department of Project Evaluation & Supervision, I have been actively involved in shaping and driving the success of the organization’s projects. I was responsible for setting up teams, guiding project leads, and leading team meetings to ensure smooth operations and goal alignment. As a fundraising coordinator, I built valuable connections with donors, organized successful fundraising events, and advised project teams on securing financial resources, contributing significantly to the organization’s growth.

Campus 4 Change has been involved in several impactful projects, including "Campus for Ukraine," which provided social and integration support to Ukrainian refugees in Munich, helping them build new connections and navigate life in the city. The "Healthcare for Change" project in Kenya focuses on improving healthcare services by implementing digital patient records and supporting local health initiatives. Furthermore, the "HIV Prevention Project" in Cameroon combines health education with digital literacy, empowering communities to raise HIV awareness while fostering essential skills. These projects reflect the organization’s commitment to addressing pressing social issues and driving sustainable change.
