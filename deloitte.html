Financial Advisory Analytics

Deloitte is a leading accounting and consulting company in Switzerland and provides industry-specific services. The Financial Advisory Analytics team mainly supports firms in the M&A process.

During my traineeship at Deloitte Switzerland in the Financial Advisory Analytics team, I specialized in business analysis and financial structuring. Working with a leading manufacturing corporation, I conducted comprehensive industry analysis using tools such as Power BI, SQL, and Microsoft Excel. My responsibilities extended to developing a value-pushdown model, utilizing Microsoft Excel, aimed at enhancing key performance indicators. This experience not only enhanced my analytical skills but also provided hands-on exposure to impactful financial projects within a dynamic corporate environment.

Furthermore, my role involved collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, including finance, operations, and IT, to gather and analyze data, identify trends, and develop actionable insights. By leveraging advanced analytics techniques, I was able to uncover hidden opportunities for cost optimization and revenue enhancement, contributing to the overall strategic objectives of the organization.

Moreover, I actively participated in client meetings and presentations, where I effectively communicated complex financial concepts and findings to key stakeholders. This experience not only sharpened my presentation skills but also deepened my understanding of client needs and expectations.

In addition to my project-specific responsibilities, I actively engaged in continuous learning and development opportunities offered by Deloitte. I participated in training sessions on advanced analytics techniques, financial modeling, and industry best practices, further enhancing my technical expertise and professional acumen.

Overall, my traineeship at Deloitte Switzerland provided me with a rich and diverse learning experience, allowing me to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios and develop practical skills that are essential for success in the field of financial advisory analytics.
